My Story

I was born in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, on March 16th, 2003. I'm the youngest among two sisters and a brother. Growing up, I switched between seven different schools. My favorite subjects were Maths, Chemistry, and Biology. When I am not doing school work, I like to read books, take photos, and edit videos.

My favorite dish is rice. When I was 5, my mother used to call me 'Ms. Rice' because of how much I loved having it. I may be doing my country a disservice by saying this since rice isn't particularly a local dish, but I also like many of the local dishes my country has to offer like Tibbs and Dorowot.

Now, studying software engineering at AAU, I've entered a world where algorithms and code reign supreme. There are countless ways software engineers can help make the world a better place, so I am very lucky to be studying it. I have not yet decided what my specialization is going to be in this field since there are too many choices, but I always do research so I can decide on what is best for me.
